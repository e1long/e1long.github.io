Lab 10 - SEO and SMO

Part A

Three SEO techiniques

1. Descriptive URLS

Using a descriptive and simple naming convention for your website helps users identify what is the main purpose of the website. If your URL contains random text or numbers such as “https://www.example.com/index.php?id_wca=470&clcp27sap” rather than “https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/pancake,” it could cause confusion between users and make them not want to visit the website (Krause, 2023). A descriptive URL link helps search engines deliver relevant results to users and allows users to have an idea of what your page content is about.

2. Optimizing Images

Image SEO is optimizing images on a website to increase rankings in unpaid search engine results (Go & Paruch, 2024). There are many ways to optimize website images to drive more traffic, such as using descriptive but concise file names, adding a descriptive alt-text, and resizing and compressing images. Doing all of the above makes it easier for Google to find and understand the images, making it more likely for Google to share them for relevant searches (Go & Paruch, 2024). Additionally, this provides accessibility for those who cannot view the image.

3. Keyword Research

Researching keywords for your website can help identify what your target audience is looking for and what keywords to use to optimize ranking opportunities (Go & Fogg, 2024). Additionally, not only does researching keywords help identify which keyword to use, but it also reveals competitors’ keywords that rank higher than your website. Knowing competitors’ keyword ranking gives you an advantage by having an opportunity to beat your competitors' ranking.


Three SMO techiniques

Adding Hashtags

Although most people know what a hashtag is and how it works, there are ways to utilize and optimize its functions. A common misconception is that the more hashtags you use, the more engagement you get. However, according to Instagram, using three to five relevant hashtags will help people discover your content on the platform through search (Tien, 2023). Knowing Instagram's best practices for using a hashtag is beneficial because it enables you to utilize the hashtag feature, allowing people to discover your content.

Optimizing Profile Bio

An optimized bio is a powerful tool to enhance discoverability and engagement on social platforms. By clearly presenting key information such as who you are, your business’s purpose, the topics you focus on, your brand tone, location, and contact details, you provide users with a compelling reason to follow you (Tien, 2023). A strong bio also incorporates strategic keywords, ensuring visibility in searches and aligning with SEO best practices. This approach not only attracts the right audience but also builds trust by offering transparency and clarity about your brand or identity.

Tagging Relevant Accounts

Tagging featured accounts, such as brands or collaborators, increases content visibility to their followers. For instance, tagging “Glossier” on a post introduces it to users who follow or search for Glossier, expanding reach and creating networking opportunities (Tien, 2023).
